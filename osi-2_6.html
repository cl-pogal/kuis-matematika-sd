🔑 Pembahasan Lengkap

1
Luas, Keliling & Biaya Keramik
a) Luas Lantai yang Dipasang Keramik
Luas ruangan = \(15 \times 10 = 150\) m²
Luas taman (persegi) = \(3 \times 3 = 9\) m²
Luas kolam (persegi panjang) = \(5 \times 2 = 10\) m²
Luas lantai = \(150 - 9 - 10 = 131\) m²
✓ 131 m²
b) Total Biaya Keramik
Biaya = \(131 \times \text{Rp}\,45.000 = \text{Rp}\,5.895.000\)
✓ Rp 5.895.000,00
c) Jumlah Kotak Keramik
Jumlah kotak = \(131 \div 0{,}5 = 262\) kotak
✓ 262 kotak
2
Soal Cerita — Pecahan & Uang
a) Uang untuk Pakaian
Pakaian = \(\dfrac{2}{5} \times \text{Rp}\,900.000 = \text{Rp}\,360.000\)
✓ Rp 360.000,00
b) Uang untuk Makanan
Sisa setelah pakaian = \(\text{Rp}\,900.000 - \text{Rp}\,360.000 = \text{Rp}\,540.000\)
Makanan = \(\dfrac{1}{3} \times \text{Rp}\,540.000 = \text{Rp}\,180.000\)
✓ Rp 180.000,00
c) Sisa Uang
Sisa = \(\text{Rp}\,540.000 - \text{Rp}\,180.000 = \text{Rp}\,360.000\)
Cek: \(360.000 + 360.000 + 180.000 = 900.000\) ✓
✓ Rp 360.000,00
3
Soal Cerita — Perbandingan Berat Badan
Pemisalan
Misalkan berat Rina = \(R\)
Berat Dika = \(2R\), Berat Tono = \(2 \times 2R = 4R\)
\(R + 2R + 4R = 7R = 140 \Rightarrow R = 20\)
a) Berat Badan Rina
Rina = \(R = 20\) kg
✓ 20 kg
b) Berat Badan Dika
Dika = \(2R = 2 \times 20 = 40\) kg
✓ 40 kg
c) Berat Badan Tono
Tono = \(4R = 4 \times 20 = 80\) kg
Cek: \(20 + 40 + 80 = 140\) ✓
✓ 80 kg
