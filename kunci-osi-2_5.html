🔢 Operasi Hitung Campuran — Mixed Operations
C Jawaban: 20
📝 Pembahasan / Solution
Hitung sesuai urutan operasi (PEMDAS/BODMAS):
\((18 \times 4 - 24) \div 6 + 5^2 - 13\)
\(= (72 - 24) \div 6 + 25 - 13\)
\(= 48 \div 6 + 25 - 13\)
\(= 8 + 25 - 13\)
\(= 8 + 12\)
\(= \) 20 ✓

🔢 Persamaan Linear — Linear Equation
B Jawaban: 7
📝 Pembahasan / Solution
Persamaan: \(A \times 15 + 120 \div 8 = 4 \times (25 + 5)\)
\(A \times 15 + 15 = 4 \times 30\)
\(A \times 15 + 15 = 120\)
\(A \times 15 = 120 - 15 = 105\)
\(A = 105 \div 15\)
\(A = \) 7 ✓

🔢 Pecahan — Fractions
D Jawaban: \(2\dfrac{11}{12}\)
📝 Pembahasan / Solution
\(2\tfrac{1}{3} + 1\tfrac{3}{4} - 1\tfrac{1}{6}\)
KPK(3, 4, 6) = 12  |  LCM(3, 4, 6) = 12
\(= \dfrac{7}{3} + \dfrac{7}{4} - \dfrac{7}{6}\)
\(= \dfrac{28}{12} + \dfrac{21}{12} - \dfrac{14}{12}\)
\(= \dfrac{28 + 21 - 14}{12} = \dfrac{35}{12}\)
\(= \) \(2\dfrac{11}{12}\) ✓

🔢 Diskon & Harga — Discount & Price

Harga awal = Rp 80.000. Diskon = 35%
Harga setelah diskon = \(80.000 \times (100\% - 35\%)\)

\(= 80.000 \times 0{,}65\)
\(= \) Rp 52.000,00 ✓

🔢 FPB — GCD (Greatest Common Divisor)
C Jawaban: 24
📝 Pembahasan / Solution
Faktorisasi prima / Prime factorization:
\(72 = 2^3 \times 3^2\)
\(96 = 2^5 \times 3\)
\(120 = 2^3 \times 3 \times 5\)
FPB = ambil faktor prima dengan pangkat terkecil yang ada di semua bilangan
FPB \(= 2^3 \times 3 = 8 \times 3 = \) 24 ✓

🔢 KPK — LCM (Least Common Multiple)
D Jawaban: 210
📝 Pembahasan / Solution
Faktorisasi prima / Prime factorization:
\(14 = 2 \times 7\)
\(21 = 3 \times 7\)
\(35 = 5 \times 7\)
KPK = ambil semua faktor prima dengan pangkat terbesar
KPK \(= 2 \times 3 \times 5 \times 7 = \) 210 ✓

🔢 KPK Aplikasi — LCM Application
B Jawaban: 36 hari
📝 Pembahasan / Solution
Faktorisasi prima / Prime factorization:
\(4 = 2^2\)
\(6 = 2 \times 3\)
\(9 = 3^2\)
KPK \(= 2^2 \times 3^2 = 4 \times 9 = \) 36 hari ✓
Ketiga orang akan bertemu kembali setelah 36 hari.

🔢 Luas Persegi Panjang — Rectangle Area
A Jawaban: 288 cm²
📝 Pembahasan / Solution
Keliling = 72 cm, maka: \(2(p + l) = 72 \Rightarrow p + l = 36\)
Panjang = 2 × lebar: \(p = 2l\)
Substitusi: \(2l + l = 36 \Rightarrow 3l = 36 \Rightarrow l = 12\) cm
Panjang: \(p = 2 \times 12 = 24\) cm
Luas \(= p \times l = 24 \times 12 = \) 288 cm² ✓

🔢 Ubin Lantai — Floor Tiles

Luas satu ubin \(= 40 \times 40 = 1.600\) cm²
Jumlah ubin \(= \dfrac{960.000}{1.600} = \) 600 ✓

🔢 Sudut Segitiga — Triangle Angles

🔢 Sudut Terbesar — Largest Angle
B Jawaban: 75°
📝 Pembahasan / Solution
Dari soal 11, \(x = 25\). Hitung ketiga sudut:
Sudut 1: \(2x = 2 \times 25 = 50°\)
Sudut 2: \(3x = 3 \times 25 = 75°\)
Sudut 3: \(x + 30 = 25 + 30 = 55°\)
Cek: \(50° + 75° + 55° = 180°\) ✓
Sudut terbesar = 75° ✓

🔢 Selisih Waktu — Time Difference
C Jawaban: 4 jam 35 menit
📝 Pembahasan / Solution
Dari pukul 06.45 sampai 11.20
06.45 → 11.45 = 5 jam (tepat)
11.45 → 11.20 = mundur 25 menit
Selisih = 5 jam − 25 menit = 4 jam 35 menit ✓
Cara lain: 11.20 − 06.45 = (11×60+20) − (6×60+45) = 680 − 405 = 275 menit = 4 jam 35 menit

🔢 Kalender — Calendar
D Jawaban: 6 Juni
📝 Pembahasan / Solution
Mulai: 8 Maret + 90 hari
Sisa hari di Maret: \(31 - 8 = 23\) hari
April: 30 hari  →  total: \(23 + 30 = 53\) hari
Mei: 31 hari  →  total: \(53 + 31 = 84\) hari
Sisa: \(90 - 84 = 6\) hari masuk Juni
Tanggal = 6 Juni ✓

🔢 Rata-rata Gabungan — Combined Average

📝 Pembahasan / Solution
Total nilai 8 siswa = \(8 \times 82 = 656\)
Total nilai 6 siswa = \(6 \times 80 = 480\)

Rata-rata 2 siswa = \(\dfrac{176}{2} = \) 88 ✓

🔢 Sistem Persamaan — System of Equations
D Jawaban: 96
📝 Pembahasan / Solution
Diketahui: \(a + b = 168\) dan \(a - b = 24\)
Jumlahkan kedua persamaan:
\((a + b) + (a - b) = 168 + 24\)
\(2a = 192\)
\(a = \) 96 ✓
Cek: \(b = 168 - 96 = 72\), maka \(a - b = 96 - 72 = 24\) ✓

Pak Hendra — Pengeluaran Bulanan / Monthly Expenses
Pak Hendra receives a monthly salary of Rp 840,000. He spends 2/7 on electronics, then 1/3 of the remainder on clothing.
a)
Berapa rupiah yang dibelanjakan untuk elektronik?
How much is spent on electronics?

Pengeluaran elektronik \(= \dfrac{2}{7} \times 840.000\)
\(= \dfrac{2 \times 840.000}{7} = \dfrac{1.680.000}{7}\)
\(= \) Rp 240.000,00 ✓
b)
Berapa rupiah yang dibelanjakan untuk pakaian?
How much is spent on clothing?

Sisa setelah elektronik \(= 840.000 - 240.000 = 600.000\)
Pengeluaran pakaian \(= \dfrac{1}{3} \times 600.000\)
\(= \) Rp 200.000,00 ✓
c)
Berapa sisa uang Pak Hendra?
How much money does Pak Hendra have left?

Sisa uang \(= 600.000 - 200.000\)
\(= \) Rp 400.000,00 ✓

Kebun Pak Budi — Garden Area / Luas Kebun
Pak Budi has a rectangular garden (15m × 10m). Inside: a square pond (4m × 4m) and a rectangular flower garden (5m × 3m). The rest will be planted with grass at Rp 35,000/m².
a)
Luas seluruh kebun?
Total area of the garden?

Luas kebun \(= 15 \times 10 = \) 150 m² ✓
b)
Luas area yang akan ditanami rumput?
Area to be planted with grass?

Luas kolam \(= 4 \times 4 = 16\) m²
Luas taman bunga \(= 5 \times 3 = 15\) m²
Luas rumput \(= 150 - 16 - 15 = \) 119 m² ✓
c)
Berapa biaya penanaman rumput?
What is the cost of planting grass?

Biaya \(= 119 \times 35.000\)
\(= \) Rp 4.165.000,00 ✓

Berat Badan — Body Weight Problem
Bima's weight is 2/5 of Candra's. Aldo's weight is 3/2 of Bima's. Their total weight is 120 kg.
Pemisalan / Let:
Misalkan berat Candra = \(C\)
Berat Bima \(= \dfrac{2}{5}C\)
Berat Aldo \(= \dfrac{3}{2} \times \dfrac{2}{5}C = \dfrac{3}{5}C\)

Persamaan total:
\(C + \dfrac{2}{5}C + \dfrac{3}{5}C = 120\)
\(\dfrac{5C}{5} + \dfrac{2C}{5} + \dfrac{3C}{5} = 120\)
\(\dfrac{10C}{5} = 120\)
\(2C = 120 \Rightarrow C = 60\)
a)
Berat badan Candra?
Candra's weight?

Candra \(= C = \) 60 kg ✓
b)
Berat badan Bima?
Bima's weight?

Bima \(= \dfrac{2}{5} \times 60 = \) 24 kg ✓
c)
Berat badan Aldo?
Aldo's weight?

Aldo \(= \dfrac{3}{2} \times 24 = \) 36 kg ✓

Verifikasi: \(60 + 24 + 36 = 120\) kg ✓

Perjalanan Kereta — Train Journey
Train departs from P at 07:20, arrives at Q at 10:50. After a 40-minute stop, it continues to R (3 hours 15 minutes journey).
a)
Lama perjalanan P ke Q?
Travel time from P to Q?

Berangkat: 07.20  |  Tiba: 10.50
07.20 → 10.20 = 3 jam
10.20 → 10.50 = 30 menit
Lama perjalanan P→Q = 3 jam 30 menit ✓
b)
Pukul berapa kereta tiba di R?
What time does the train arrive at R?

Tiba di Q: 10.50
Berhenti 40 menit → berangkat dari Q: \(10.50 + 40\text{ menit} = 11.30\)
Perjalanan Q→R: 3 jam 15 menit
Tiba di R: \(11.30 + 3\text{ jam } 15\text{ menit} = \) 14.45 ✓
c)
Total waktu perjalanan P ke R (termasuk berhenti)?
Total travel time from P to R (including stop)?

P→Q: 3 jam 30 menit
Berhenti di Q: 40 menit
Q→R: 3 jam 15 menit
Total = \(3\text{j }30\text{m} + 40\text{m} + 3\text{j }15\text{m}\)
\(= 6\text{ jam } 85\text{ menit}\)
\(= 6\text{ jam } + 1\text{ jam } 25\text{ menit}\)
\(= \) 7 jam 25 menit ✓

Barisan Bilangan — Number Sequence
Sequence: 3, 8, 15, 24, 35, ... Find the pattern and determine specific terms.
Pola / Pattern:
Barisan: 3, 8, 15, 24, 35, ...
Rumus suku ke-\(n\): \(T_n = n \times (n+2)\)
Verifikasi:
\(T_1 = 1 \times 3 = 3\) ✓  |  \(T_2 = 2 \times 4 = 8\) ✓  |  \(T_3 = 3 \times 5 = 15\) ✓
\(T_4 = 4 \times 6 = 24\) ✓  |  \(T_5 = 5 \times 7 = 35\) ✓
a)
Suku ke-10?
The 10th term?

\(T_{10} = 10 \times (10 + 2) = 10 \times 12 = \) 120 ✓
b)
Jumlah digit suku ke-10?
Sum of digits of the 10th term?

Suku ke-10 = 120
Jumlah digit = \(1 + 2 + 0 = \) 3 ✓
c)
Suku ke-15?
The 15th term?

\(T_{15} = 15 \times (15 + 2) = 15 \times 17 = \) 255 ✓
